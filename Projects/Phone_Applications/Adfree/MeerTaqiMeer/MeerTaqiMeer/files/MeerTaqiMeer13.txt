Be-Khudii le gaii kahaan ham ko
 
be-Khudii le gaii kahaan ham ko
der se intazaar hai apanaa
 
rote phirate hain saarii-saarii raat
ab yahii rozagaar hai apanaa
 
de ke dil ham jo ho gae majabuur
is mai kyaa iKhtiyaar hai apanaa
 
kuchh nahiin ham misaal-e-anqaa lek
shahar-shahar ishtahaar hai apanaa
 
jis ko tum aasmaan kahate ho
sou dilon kaa Gubaar hai apanaa
