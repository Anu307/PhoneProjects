Baat kya admi ki ban ai

Baat kya admi ki ban ai

asman se zamin napwai

 

charakh zan uske waste hai madam

ho gaya din tamam rat ai

 

mah-o-khurshid-o-bad sabhi

uski khatir hue hain saudai

 

kaise kaise kiye taraddad jab

rang rang usko chiz pahunchai

 

usko tarajah sab ke upar de

lutf-e-haq ne ki izzat afzai

 

hairat ati hai uski baten dekh

khud sari khud satai khud rai

 

shukr ke sajdon mein ye wajib tha

ye bhi karta sajda jabin sai

 

so to uski tabiyat-e-sarkash

sar na lai faro ke tuk lai

 

'Mir' na chiz musht-e-khak Allah

un ne ye kibriya kahan pai
