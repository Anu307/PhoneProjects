Jite-ji kucha-e-dildar se jaya na gaya
 
jite-ji kucha-e-dildar se jaya na gaya
us ki diwar ka sar se mere saya na gaya
 
dil k tain atish-e-hijran se bachaya na gaya
ghar jala samne par ham se bujhaya na gaya
 
kya tunak hausala the dida-o-dil apne ah
ek dam raz mohabbat ka chupaya na gaya
 
dil jo didar ka qayel ki bahot bhuka tha
is sitam-kushta se yak zakhm bhi khaya na gaya
 
shahar-e-dil ah ajab jaye thi par usk gaye
aisa ujra k kisi tarah basaya na gaya
