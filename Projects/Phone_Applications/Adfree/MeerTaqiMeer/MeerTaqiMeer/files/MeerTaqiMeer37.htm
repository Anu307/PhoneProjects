Yaar bin talkh zindgani thi

 

Yaar bin talkh zindgani thi

dosti muddai-e-jani thi

 

sheb mein fayda tammul ka

sochna tab tha hab jawani thi

 

mere qisse se sab ki ninden gain

kuch ajab taur ki kahani thi

 

ku-e-qatil se bach k nikla khizr

isi mein us ki zindagani thi

 

fitr par bhi tha 'Mir' k ik rang

kafani pahni to zafarani thi
