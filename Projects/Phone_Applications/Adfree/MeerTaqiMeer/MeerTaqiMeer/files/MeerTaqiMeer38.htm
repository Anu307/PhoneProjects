Mir' darya hai, sune sher zabani us ki

Allah Allah re tabiyat ki rawani us ki

 

minh to bochar ka dekha hai barste tumne

isi andaz se thi ashk-fishani us ki

 

bat ki tarz ko dekho to koi jadu tha

par mili khak mein sab sahar bayani us ki

 

marsi-e-dil k kai kah k diye logon ne

shahr-e-dili mein hai sab pas nishani us ki

 

ab gaye us k jo afasos nahin kuchh hasil

haif sad haif ki kuchh qadr na jani us ki
