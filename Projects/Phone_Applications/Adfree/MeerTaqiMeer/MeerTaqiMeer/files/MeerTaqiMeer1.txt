Andoh Se Hui Na Rihai Tamam Shab
andoh se hui na rihai tamam shab
mujh dil jale ko nind na ai tamam shab
 
chamak chali gai thi sitaron ki subah tak
k asman se didabarai tamam shab
 
jab main ne shuru qissa kiya ankhen khol din
yaqini thi mujh ko chashm numai tamam shab
 
waqt-e-siyah ne der mein kal yawari si ki
thi dushmanon se in ki larai tamam shab
 
tare se teri palkon pe qatre the ashk k
de rahe hain 'Mir' dikhai tamam shab
