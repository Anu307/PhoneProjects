Arzuen hazar rakhte hain

 

Arzuen hazar rakhte hain

to bhi ham dil ko mar rakhte hain

 

barq kam hausla hai ham bhi to

dil ek beqarar rakhte hain

 

gair hai murad-e-inayat hae

ham bhi to tum se pyar rakhte hain

 

na nigah na payam na wada

nam ko ham bhi yar rakhte hain

 

ham se khush zam-zama kahan yun to

lab-o-lahja hazar rakhte hain

 

chote dil k hain butan mashur

bas yahi aitbar rakhte hain

 

phir bhi karte hain 'Mir' sahib ishq

hain jawan ikhtiyar rakhte hain
