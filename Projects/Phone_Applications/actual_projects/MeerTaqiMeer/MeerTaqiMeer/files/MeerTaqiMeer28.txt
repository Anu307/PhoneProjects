jo tu hi sanam ham se bezar hoga
to jina hamain apna dushawar hoga
 
gam-e-hijr rakhega betab dil ko
hamain kurhte-kurhte kuch azar hoga
 
jo afrat-e-ulfat hai aisa to ashiq
koi din main barson ka bimar hoga
 
uchtati mulaqat kab tak rahegi
kabhu to tah-e-dil se bhi yar hoga
 
tujhe dekh kar lag gaya dil na jana
k is sangdil se hamain pyar hoga
