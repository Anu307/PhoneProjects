Barang-e-bu-e-gul, is bagh k ham ashna hote

 

barang-e-bu-e-gul, is bagh k ham ashna hote

k ham-rah-e-saba tuk sair karte, aur hawa hote

 

sarapa arzu hone ne banda kar diya hamko

wagarna ham khuda the, gar dil-e-bemudda hote

 

falak, ai kash! hamko khak hi rakhta, ki us mein ham

gubar-e-rah hote ya kisi ki khak-e-pa hote

 

ilahi kaise hote hain jinhen hai bandagi khwahish

hamein to sharm daman-gar hoti hai, khuda hote

 

kahen jo kuch malamatgar, baja hai 'Mir' kya jane

unhen malum tab hota, ki waise se juda hote
