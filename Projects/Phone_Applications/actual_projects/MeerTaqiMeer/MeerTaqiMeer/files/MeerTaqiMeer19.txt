Mahar ki tujhse tawaqqo thi sitamgar nikla
 
mahar ki tujhse tawaqqo thi sitamgar nikla
mom samajhe the tere dil ko so paththar nikla
 
dag hun rashk-e-mohabbat se k itna betab
kis ki taskin ke liye ghar se tu bahar nikla
 
jite ji ah tere kuche se koi na phira
jo sitamdida raha jake so mar kar nikla
 
dil ki abadi ki is had hai kharabi k na puch
jana jata hai ki is rah se lashkar nikla
 
ham ne jana tha likhega tu koi harf ae 'Mir'
par tera nama to ek shauq ka daftar nikla
